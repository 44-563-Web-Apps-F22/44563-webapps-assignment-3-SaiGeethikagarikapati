The Indian Museum

Some of the must watch things are Mughal Paintings and collections of antiques

Travel directions

The nearest airport to the indian museum is Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport

  1. Head south on Jawaharlal Nehru Rd toward Kyd St
  2. Take Mirza Ghalib St to Dharamtala St/Lenin Sarani Rd in Esplanade :
    1. Turn left onto Kyd St
    2. Turn right onto Dharamtala St/Lenin Sarani Rd $ Turn left after Matilal & Co.
  3. Continue onto Acharya Prafulla Chandra Rd/Sealdah Flyover
  4. Keep right to continue on Kestopur - Baguiati Flyover

Some of the other locations around the museum to watch are :

  • Taj Mahal
  • Rathambore National park
  • Hawa Mahal
  • Qutab Minar
